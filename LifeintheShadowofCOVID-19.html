About

Since the COVID-19 pandemic officially began in Wuhan in mid-january, it has now rapidly spread across the globe, bringing entire economies and nations to a grinding halt. This website is a blog where individuals can post either pictures or stories of how the COVID-19 outbreak has disrupted their lives and daily routines. We hope that someday, this website will be a historical record for how teh Coronavirus impacted our society, and how pandemics can impact our increasingly interconnected society in the future.

Photos

Empty store shelves, empty streets, closed signs, health warnings, chances are you have already seen a lot of this, not only on social media, but most definetely in your own life as well. Here you can upload your own photos of your daily life during Quarantine, and even videos of your life during the Quarantine.
